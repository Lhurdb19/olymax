Faqs

Frequently Asked Questions

What file formats do you accept for printing?

Most printing services prefer PDF files, but it's crucial to clarify accepted formats to ensure compatibility. Providing guidelines helps clients submit print-ready files.

What is the recommended resolution for images in print files?

Understanding the optimal image resolution ensures clarity in the final print. Typically, 300 dots per inch (DPI) is the industry standard for high-quality prints.

How do I choose the right paper stock for my project?

Different paper stocks impact the look and feel of printed materials. A printing service should offer guidance on paper options based on factors like project type, budget, and desired finish.

What is the turnaround time for printing projects?

Knowing the expected turnaround time is crucial for planning marketing campaigns or events. Clear communication about production timelines helps manage expectations and deadlines.

Do you offer proofing services before finalizing the print job?

A reliable printing service should provide proofing options to review and approve designs before mass production. This step ensures accuracy and allows for necessary adjustments before the final print run.
